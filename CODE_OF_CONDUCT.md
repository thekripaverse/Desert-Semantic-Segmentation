# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainer.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove or edit contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
